WHAT WE DO

OpenFuture Charity and Foundational Trust is dedicated to transforming lives through its comprehensive educational initiatives aimed at empowering underprivileged children and youth. Through a combination of literacy programs, vocational training, scholarships, and mentorship initiatives, OpenFuture Charity and Foundational Trust ensures that individuals from marginalized communities have access to quality education and skill development opportunities. The organization’s education services align with the goals of the New Education Policy (2020) and Sustainable Development Goal , which promotes inclusive, equitable, and lifelong learning for all. OpenFuture Charity and Foundational Trust works closely with children and young people aged 3 to 18 from economically challenged backgrounds, including differently-abled children, those affected by natural disasters, abandoned or street-connected youth, and children from remote tribal regions and underserved rural areas.

Our Child Education Program

Our mission is to empower children through holistic education. We focus on critical support areas like literacy, vocational training, scholarships, and mentorship to enable long-term, sustainable impact.

Literacy Programs

Our literacy initiatives ensure foundational education for all children, especially in underserved regions, promoting lifelong learning from an early age.

Vocational Training

We offer skill-based training to help children transition into productive adulthood by equipping them with practical tools for employment.

Scholarships

Financial aid ensures talented and deserving students are not denied education due to economic hardship, empowering their academic dreams.

Mentorship Initiatives

Mentorship bridges knowledge gaps, offering guidance and support from role models to help students navigate school and career pathways.
